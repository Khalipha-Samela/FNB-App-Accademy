About The Avengers

Earth's Mightiest Heroes

The Avengers are a team of extraordinary individuals with superhuman abilities who come together to protect Earth from threats too large for any one hero to handle. Originally conceived by S.H.I.E.L.D. director Nick Fury, the Avengers Initiative brings together the world's most powerful heroes to form an unparalleled defense force.

Our Mission

When danger threatens the world, the Avengers assemble to combat forces that no single hero could withstand. From alien invasions to global catastrophes to super-villain alliances, we stand as Earth's first and last line of defense.

Avengers Assemble

The team's famous battle cry "Avengers Assemble!" has become a rallying call for heroes across the universe. Though membership has changed over time, our core mission remains the same: to fight the battles that humanity cannot fight alone.
